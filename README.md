# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Analysis
**Tools:** PostgreSQL · Google, CLaudeAI  
**Dataset:** UCI Online Retail Dataset · 541,909 transactions · Dec 2010–Dec 2011

## Business Problem
A UK-based e-commerce business had no visibility into which customers were 
driving revenue — creating an invisible retention risk with no early warning system.

## Key Findings
- 🔴 Top 10% of customers (434 buyers) drive **61.4% of total revenue**
- 📈 November 2011 was peak month at **£1.16M** — revenue grew 103% from Feb low
- 🌍 UK = 82% of revenue across 38 markets — international is real but underdeveloped
- ⚠️ Single customer #14646 generated **£280K** — dangerous single-point dependency

## Recommendation
Implement a VIP retention program targeting the top 434 customers immediately. 
Losing 50 of them could wipe out £500K+ in annual revenue.
